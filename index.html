Our Story

@ Fictional part

Take one step forward and get to know these great buddies.

Even though Retrievers look always smiling and unconcerned, they have a very big mission on the Earth too - bringing happiness to everyone!

The secret team of Retrievers all around the world are hiding their secret mission and walking around streets with their human families like they do know nothing :)

Once a month this secret team come together to party! They know how to get fun for sure.

Let us invite you to their party. You will be their best friends and learn more about their big mission in the world.

@ Factual part

Dogs are our best companions, always by our side no matter what ups and downs life brings. We love them as much as they love us.

We started with idea that it is our responsibility as adults to raise and teach our kids to love animals.

And we aim to do this in a fun way to attract children to discover more - the Retriver Party!

The Retriever Party will be released both as a children's book and adventure video series on digital channels soon.

The story will be focusing on the Retrievers' secret mission of how they bring happiness to people.

While they always bring us happiness, we gotta give back, right?

We want to see our best buddies always healthy and happy. That's why we will be donating to Canine Health Foundations thanks to the Retriever Party NFT project.
